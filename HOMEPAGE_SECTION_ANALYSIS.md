## ğŸ¨ Design Specifications

### Modern, Enterprise-Level Design:

#### **Color Palette:**
- Primary: Your brand colors
- Accent: Vibrant gradient (e.g., blue to purple)
- Background: Soft gradient or subtle pattern
- Text: High contrast for readability

#### **Typography:**
- Headline: Bold, 48-56px, modern sans-serif
- Body: 18-20px, readable line height (1.6-1.8)
- Feature titles: 24px, semi-bold

#### **Spacing:**
- Section padding: 120px top/bottom (desktop), 60px (mobile)
- Content max-width: 1200px
- Grid gaps: 40-60px

#### **Animations:**
- Fade-in on scroll (Framer Motion)
- Parallax effect on image
- Hover effects on feature cards
- Smooth transitions (0.3-0.5s)

#### **Unique Elements:**
- **Glassmorphism**: Frosted glass effect on stat badges
- **Gradient Mesh**: Animated background gradient
- **3D Tilt**: Subtle 3D effect on hover
- **Floating Elements**: Decorative shapes with parallax
- **Progress Indicators**: Animated skill bars or circular progress
